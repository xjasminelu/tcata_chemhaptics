Please make sure your setup is ready by following these step-by-step instructions.


1. Fill your pipette.

To fill your pipette, squeeze the top and submerge the tip in the chemical liquid. Release the top so that a vacuum is created and the top fills with liquid.

2. Insert pipette tip into silicone patch.

Press pipette so that its tip goes through the marked hole. The end of the tip of the pipette should just barely reach to the open channels.

3. Put on silicon patch (with pipette inserted).

Slide arm through elastic so that patch is resting on your inner forearm. The open channels side of silicone patch should be facing skin.

4. Tighten elastic bands to form seal with skin. (Important!)

Use white beads to tighten elastic bands so that silicone patch has a good seal with skin in order for liquid to pass through without leaking.

5. Prepare to squeeze pipette.

On the next screen, we will be asking you to slowly dispel the liquid into the open channels and suck it back. You can think of it as generating a continuous flow.

6. Prepare to check/uncheck sensations as you feel them.

On the next screen, we will start the timer and ask you to check and uncheck sensations as you feel them.

Please check and uncheck the sensations listed below as you feel them.

You can also use your keyboard to check/uncheck boxes of sensations.

Please follow these steps and proceed to next phase.


1. Suck liquid back into pipette.

To fill your pipette, squeeze the top and slowly release. You should see the liquid return to the pipette top.

2. Carefully remove silicon patch.

Slide off the patch and rest to the side. You can dispel liquid into trash after the trial is over.

3. Wipe off any excess liquid.

Use a dry napkin to wipe off any excess liquid left behind.

Congrats! You've finished the trial.
